What is $ESOR?

$ESOR is a pioneering memecoin on the Oasis Protocol. Designed to be the first mover, and to appeal to true explorers of the Blockchain, it's a hidden gem that's one of it's kind. Still, thanks to Illuminex Crosschain swaps, it's surprisingly accesible to most traders!

How to buy $ESOR?

$ESOR is listed on IlluminexOasis.

If you already have ROSE on Oasis, you can skip directly to step 6.

  1. Get a MetaMask wallet.
  2. Obtain USDT or ETH on the Ethereum network or BNB on the Binance Smart Chain.
  3. Go to the Illuminex swap page and connect your wallet.
  4. In the Send box, select USDT, ETH or BNB.
  5. Paste the Esor contract address into the token selection box in the Swap and Get box, then select ESOR.
  6. Choose an amount and then press the Swap button to get some ESOR.
